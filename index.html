Advanced strategies and car mechanics

Once you get past the beginner phase of just trying to hit the ball, the game gets incredibly deep. You need to learn how to manage your momentum. Don't just hold the gas down the entire match. Feathers the throttle. Tap the brakes to let the opponent fly past the ball, then take the open shot.

Wall play is essential. The arena has curved edges, meaning the ball will roll up the wall if hit hard enough. Driving up the wall and jumping off to hit an aerial shot is incredibly difficult to defend against. Most beginners stay on the ground. If you learn to play in the air, you will win almost every match.

Also, don't ignore your teammates if you're playing a team mode. Sometimes the best move is to hang back near your own goal and act as a goalie while the AI chases the ball. The AI is aggressive, but it's not smart. It will center the ball for you if you're patient.
